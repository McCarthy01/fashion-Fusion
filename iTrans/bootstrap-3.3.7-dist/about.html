Who are we?

Design Fusion is an initiative born out of the need to solve the cumbersome process of getting beautiful and finished designs within the stipulated period, thus avoiding the constant disappointments and bad tailoring that fashion lovers often complain about.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform where fashion lovers can meet with some of the best designers in the fashion industry for the creation of their desired styles. We also aim to achieve SDG goals of the country through creation of job opportunities, innovation and the export of native African fabrics and attires to other parts of the world.

Our Vision

We have a vision to ensure a steady and consistent distribution of native African prints and fabrics, both within and outside Nigeria. We also strive to achieve and promote the "Buy Nigeria, Grow Nigeria" initiative.

How Are We Different From Other Brands?

  • We provide an unrestricted access to some of the best designs and designers on the planet.
  • We provide a direct export of quality African fabrics to other parts of the world.
  • Our platform is one that creates a structured process for all of our clients.
  • We offer follow-up services which include a 6-months adjustment period for clients who experience difficulty fitting in their designs as a result of body changes.
  • We provide three modes of getting measurements from our clients, so as to ensure that they get exactly what they have requested.
